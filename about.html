Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Technology

Gbogolee Tech was founded with a single conviction: that premium technology services should not be limited to large corporations or foreign brands. Nigerians deserve access to world-class tech support, beautiful design, and reliable software — built by people who understand the local landscape.

What began as a small repair operation grew into a full-service tech brand offering everything from sleek website design and custom software to logo creation and professional consultations. Every client who walked through our door fuelled our drive to be better, faster, and more creative.

"We don't just fix gadgets or build websites — we invest in your vision and deliver results that speak for themselves."

— Oladejo Oluwagbogo Isabela, Founder

Today, Gbogolee Tech is a trusted name for individuals, startups, schools, and businesses across Nigeria who demand quality, speed, and genuine care in every project.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and premium technology services that empower Nigerian individuals and businesses to compete at a global level — through innovation, quality craftsmanship, and dedicated support.

Our Vision

To become West Africa's most trusted technology service brand — a company known for transforming ideas into impactful digital realities, and for raising the bar of tech excellence on the African continent.

Our Values

Integrity in every interaction. Excellence in every deliverable. Creativity without compromise. We treat every client's project as if it were our own business on the line.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings
to Premium Brand

Every milestone shaped Gbogolee Tech into the brand it is today.

2021
The Spark — Phone Repair Service

Started with a passion for fixing smartphones and laptops, building a reputation for fast, affordable, and reliable hardware repair in the local community.

2022
Expanding into Design

Added logo, flyer, and graphics design to the service portfolio. The brand identity work attracted new business clients seeking professional visual communication.

Mid 2022
First Website Launched for a Client

Delivered the first full business website — a turning point that opened the door to web design and development as a core service offering.

2023
CBT System Development Launched

Built and deployed the first Computer-Based Testing system for a secondary school, establishing Gbogolee Tech as a serious software development provider in the education sector.

2024
Full Digital Brand — 100+ Clients

Crossed the 100-client milestone. Formalized digital branding and tech consultation services, and launched the official Gbogolee Tech brand portfolio.

Today
Growing Stronger — 12 Services & Counting

Gbogolee Tech now offers 12 premium tech services, serving clients across Nigeria with world-class quality, and continuing to build the future of African tech.
